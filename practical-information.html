Careers

First of all, thank you for your interest in working at Goldcrest Games. At the moment we are not searching for any more members for our team. We will update this page the moment we are. However, you are free to send in an open sollicitation to info@goldcrestgames.com. In your email, please state the following:
  • What role you would like to perform and why
  • Why you would like to work at Goldcrest Games
  • What makes you the best candidate for this role
Please also attach your portfolio and resume, so that we can check a look before meeting you. Additionally, it is recommended to sign up for our newsletter as we will post any job interviews there if the time comes.

Internships

As we are just a small game studio of three people, we unfortunately do not have the manpower to hire any interns at our company. However, just like applying for a full-time job here, you are very welcome to send in an open sollicitation to info@goldcrestgames.com, so that when we are looking for people, we can contact you. In your email, please state the following:
  • What role you would like to perform and why
  • Why you would like to work at Goldcrest Games
  • What makes you the best candidate for this role
  • What you would like to do for your school assignment here
  • Your school and study
Please also attach your portfolio and resume, so that we can check a look before meeting you. Additionally, it is recommended to sign up for our newsletter as we will post any job interviews there if the time comes.

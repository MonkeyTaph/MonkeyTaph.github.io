About

Goldcrest Games is an indie game studio located in Groningen and was founded by Bas Altenburg, Justin Geschiere and Evalien Wiersma in 2016. Among other reasons, we mainly started the company to create an environment in which we could practice our skills, experiment with new techniques and, of course, make games that both entertain and inspire its players.
Most of our games are meant to be played by friends who like to spend an afternoon joking around in a silly game especially made for them. That's why most of our games involve violently, yet comically, battling your friends in unlikely scenarios. In our eyes, there's no better way to show your love for your friends!
Although at the moment we think our team is nice and cozy, we think that our game studio should grow. This enables us to offer our players the best experiences within the game and its community.

If you would like to keep up to date, please follow our social media or join our news letter!
